Description

A crisp, refreshing mix of fresh vegetables tossed in a light vinaigrette. This simple salad is the perfect side dish or light meal, offering a clean, healthy taste with every bite.

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Carrot
  • Lettuce
  • Green Pepper
  • Tomato
  • Red onion
  • Lemon Juice
  • Honey or sugar
  • Salt & pepper
  • Mustard
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Wash and Prepare Veggies
  2. Make the Dressing
  3. Assemble the Salad
  4. Toss and Serve
